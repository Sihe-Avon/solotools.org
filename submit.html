Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about submitting a tool

What kind of tools can I submit?

We accept AI-powered tools that are useful for solo entrepreneurs and one-person businesses. The tool should help with productivity, content creation, marketing, development, design, customer support, or other business functions.

How long does the review process take?

We typically review submissions within 5-7 business days. If your tool is approved, you'll receive an email notification when it's added to our directory.

Is there a fee to submit a tool?

No, submitting a tool to our directory is completely free. We aim to create a comprehensive resource for solo entrepreneurs without any barriers.

Can I submit my own tool?

Yes, you can submit your own tool. Just be transparent about your relationship to the tool in the submission form. We welcome tools from creators and teams of all sizes.
